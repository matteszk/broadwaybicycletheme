Stand Rental

Access to our tools and the truing stands.

Stand rental and instruction hours are subject to stand and mechanic availability, as well as holiday hours. Call ahead to confirm.

Some speciality tools are only available with instruction and incur instruction hourly rate. Some tools are not available at all. Call ahead if you need a speciality tool.

Hours for Stand Rental & Instruction

Monday–Wednesday 10–4
Thursday 11–4:30
Friday 11–4
Saturday 10:30–5:00
Sunday 12:30–4:00

Rates

Without Instruction $24/hour
With Instruction $48/hour

Rates are charged in 5 minute increments.

Things to know about stand rental

  • You get access to all the basic tools and the truing stands
  • All rates are charged in 5 minute increments
  • Certain specialty tools are not available and some are but may require supervision at the teaching per-hour rate.
  • Stand rental and instruction is subject to availability of stands and/or mechanics
