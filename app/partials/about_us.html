Worker Owned Since 1972

We‘re a collectively run bicycle shop in operation for 40+ years. A subset of workers own and democratically manage the business. We are proud to offer a minimum wage of $15 per hour and health insurance for full time employees.

Walking into a bicycle shop for the first time can be an intimidating endeavor for many, which is why Broadway strives to create a welcoming environment for all persons that come through our door.

Broadway regularly sponsors, volunteers, and donates to local organizations and events including:

  • Mass Bike Silent Auction
  • Bikes Not Bombs Vocational Training Program
  • Bikes Not Bombs Bike-A-Thon
  • Broadway’s Bike to Work Week Pancake Breakfast
  • Boston Bicycle Reflector Zine
  • Alternatives Community and Environment (A.C.E.) Silent Auction
  • 10% Discount to Brattle Theater members
  • Celebrating Gay Pride Week by hosting a bake sale with all proceed going to Boston GLASS
